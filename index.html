Fruit Preparation: Meet Your 5-A-Day Demand!



Fruit is an important part of our diet. It contains lots of vitamins and minerals. Nature has blessed us with a variety in different colours that depict the richness of each type. The different vitamins in our fruits include vitamins A, B, C, E, K etc. Minerals fruits include calcium, iron, manganese, potassium, magnesium etc.


A popular saying that 'an apple a day, keeps the doctor away' is justified by the benefits of eating fruit as part of our diet. Among the benefits is building strong bones and teeth and blood pressure regulation. Other benefits include proper heart functioning, nerve transmissions and muscle contration, eyes and skin health, protection against infections, healing of cuts and wounds, healthy teeth gums and so on.
